We are a small independent team of 4 students based in Prague, Czechia. We are creating games in our free time and we hope to release one in the near future.
The story behind our name is not as deep as you might think. We were brainstorming for hours, days and weeks to come up with a name for our new dev team and there was that one sentence that stuck with us throughout the whole thinking process: "The name you choose has to be really original". So naturally, whenever someone came up with a cool name, someone else searched the internet and found ten other companies with that name making it not original. And as we kept thinking, searching and rejecting, one of us just lost it and said: "Original? All names are taken already except like...I don't know...The Walking Dumpling or something...". And we just sat there completely silent looking at each other with a thought that we just might have came up with the best company name ever created.
No, we haven't. We do everything in our free time, we pay everything on our own and the process of game dev is very difficult - especially being inexperienced like we are. But we are currently working on 3 projects - Museum Mania, Apocalypseburg and our Roblox project Chalice. A release date for any of these is not set yet.
We find this way of working much more efficient than it seems to be. When we work hard on one of the games and get tired from it, we switch to another one and keep working there. Also, our Roblox project was meant to be an experiment but we decided to make it into a real game. When we build a bigger community and get to know what the people like, we will stick to just one game only to get it done as quickly as possible.
