Let's Celebrate!

Having experience with developing a medieval times based game in Roblox (Chalice) The Walking Dumpling developers and community as well are excited for the release of the sequel to the famous historical game, that being Kingdom Come: Deliverance II.

The game is made by Czech developers and is set in Czech Republic. And because the developers in The Walking Dumpling Studios come from Czech republic we thought it would be a good idea to give you some thoughts on the game. For example we recorded a conversation between two developers disscusing the sound of the Kingdom Come forest. In the background you can hear a variety of birds chirping and singing. The two developers agreed that the birds sound a lot like the actual czech birds in the czech forests. This was a heartwarming surprise for our team and we hope you learned something new and interesting about the Czech republic.

The game without a doubt has stirred some excitement in the gaming community and The Walking Dumpling Team was looking on ways to celebrate this event. And what better way to celebrate the release than to join our discord and have chat with like-minded individuals and the game developers as well! The Walking Dumpling discord is open to conversations about the game and the release of it. If enough people turn up, the developers are gonna open a new channel for these disscusions. Traditionally our logo has also been changed to celebrate and set the atmosphere on fire with excitement.

So what are you still waiting for? Join The Walking Dumpling discord now and come have a chat with us!
